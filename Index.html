Joy Mega Trader was founded with a single promise — to move goods safely, on time, and across borders without compromise. From modest beginnings in Dar es Salaam, we have grown into one of East and Central Africa's most reliable cargo and logistics partners.

Our team brings deep regional knowledge, certified safety standards, and a modern fleet built for every terrain and cargo type — serving clients in 11 countries with consistency and care.
